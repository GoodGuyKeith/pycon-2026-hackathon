## Slide 2: Problem

Events overload people.

- Too many parallel sessions.
- Too much room movement.
- Awkward networking pressure.
- No easy way to remember what you actually did.

## Slide 3: Solution

Turn an event agenda into a human map.

- Recommended sessions.
- Protected breaks.
- Buddy-code networking.
- Room-code attendance.
- Venue transfer guidance.
